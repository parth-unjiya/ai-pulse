    1A Verifiable Search Is Not a Learnable Chain-of-Thought (HF Papers)

    This research paper challenges the assumption that any task solvable by a short program can be taught to a model as its chain-of-thought. This matters because it highlights the limitations of current Large Language Models (LLMs) and their ability to learn and replicate complex tasks. The findings affect AI researchers and developers who rely on LLMs for various applications, and it connects to the broader AI landscape by emphasizing the need for more advanced and nuanced models.
