The Verge reports on ClawCon, a meetup in New York City celebrating OpenClaw, an open-source AI project. Expect to see more of these community-driven AI events and projects emerge, potentially challenging the dominance of closed-source models in specific niches. The key will be whether these communities can sustain momentum and attract the necessary resources to compete effectively.
