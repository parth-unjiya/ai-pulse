    3EU Reveals Tech Sovereignty Package (European Union)

    The European Union's tech sovereignty package aims to strengthen its homegrown tech footprint by investing in European startups and companies. This move is expected to have a significant impact on the global tech industry, as the EU seeks to reduce its reliance on foreign technologies and promote local innovation. The package will likely lead to increased funding and support for European tech companies, allowing them to compete more effectively with global giants like Google and Microsoft. As the EU continues to invest in its tech industry, we can expect to see more European companies emerging as major players in the global tech landscape.
