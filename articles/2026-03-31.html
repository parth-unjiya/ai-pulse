    2Claude Code Source Code Leaked (The Decoder)

    The source code of Claude Code, a popular AI coding tool, has been leaked via a map file in their NPM registry. This leak exposes over 500,000 lines of TypeScript code, potentially allowing developers to create custom modifications and improvements. The leak has sparked a discussion on the Hacker News community, with some developers already rebuilding the executable from the leaked sourcemap. This incident highlights the importance of security and intellectual property protection in the AI industry. As a result, we can expect to see more emphasis on security measures and potential legal actions to protect AI technologies.

    3Nvidia Invests $2 Billion in Marvell Technology (AI Business)

    Nvidia has invested $2 billion in Marvell Technology to integrate XPU chips with NVLink technology. This investment aims to enhance Nvidia's AI capabilities and provide a competitive edge in the market. The integration of XPU chips with NVLink technology will enable faster data transfer and improved performance in AI applications. This move demonstrates Nvidia's commitment to advancing AI technologies and expanding its presence in the industry. As a result, we can expect to see more innovative AI solutions and increased competition in the market.
