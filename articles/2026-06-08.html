    2NVIDIA and LG Group Build an AI Factory to Advance Physical AI, Mobility and AI Infrastructure (NVIDIA Blog)

    The partnership between NVIDIA and LG Group to build an AI factory is a strategic move to accelerate the development of AI-driven businesses, including robotics, autonomous driving, and data center technologies. This collaboration will provide LG Group with access to NVIDIA's accelerated computing infrastructure, enabling the company to train, simulate, and validate AI models more efficiently. The AI factory will also facilitate the development of new AI-powered products and services, potentially giving LG Group a competitive edge in the market. As NVIDIA continues to expand its ecosystem, this partnership reinforces the company's position as a leader in the AI hardware and software space.

    3For the 2nd time in weeks, Microsoft packages laced with credential stealer (Ars Technica)

    The discovery of Microsoft packages containing a self-replicating credential stealer is a concerning development, highlighting the vulnerability of AI systems to cyber threats. This incident is the second in recent weeks, emphasizing the need for Microsoft to enhance its security measures and protect its users from potential attacks. The fact that these packages can be opened by an AI agent raises questions about the security protocols in place for AI systems and the potential risks associated with their increasing autonomy. As the use of AI becomes more widespread, it is essential for companies like Microsoft to prioritize security and develop more robust defenses against cyber threats.
