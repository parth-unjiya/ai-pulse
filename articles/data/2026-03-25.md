        → **Anthropic**'s finding that AI isn't replacing jobs yet, but is instead creating a growing skills gap, highlights the need for workers to develop new skills to remain relevant in an AI-driven economy. This development is important because it challenges the common narrative that AI will inevitably lead to widespread job displacement. Instead, Anthropic's research suggests that AI is creating new opportunities for "power users" who can leverage AI tools to enhance their productivity and capabilities. As the AI skills gap continues to grow, companies and governments will need to invest in education and training programs that equip workers with the skills necessary to thrive in an AI-driven workforce.
