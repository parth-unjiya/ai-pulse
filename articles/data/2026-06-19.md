This development is crucial because it moves beyond simplistic success-rate metrics, providing a more nuanced understanding of policy capabilities. By comprising 26 diverse tasks annotated along 5 capability dimensions and 4 generalization dimensions, **EBench** offers a comprehensive framework for assessing policy performance. This affects researchers and developers in the robotics and AI communities, as it sets a new standard for policy evaluation.
