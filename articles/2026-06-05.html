    1Google to Pay SpaceX $920M per Month (TechCrunch)

    Google's unexpected demand for its AI products has led to a significant deal with SpaceX, where Google will pay $920 million per month for compute capacity at xAI data centers. This partnership highlights the growing need for robust computing infrastructure to support AI development and deployment. The deal's value, $920 million per month for 32 months, underscores the substantial investment Google is making in its AI endeavors. This move will likely impact the competitive dynamics in the AI sector, with other companies potentially seeking similar partnerships to keep pace with Google's advancements. As Google prepares for a planned IPO, this investment in compute capacity positions the company for further growth in the AI market.
