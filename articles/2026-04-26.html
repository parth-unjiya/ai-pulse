This move is expected to enhance the performance of GPT-5.5, particularly in agentic coding tasks, while reducing token usage. The integration of Codex into GPT-5.5 will likely have a substantial impact on developers who rely on OpenAI's models for coding tasks. As the AI landscape continues to evolve, this development may influence the competitive dynamics between OpenAI and other players in the market. With this change, developers can expect more efficient and effective coding capabilities from GPT-5.5, but they will need to adapt their workflows to maximize the benefits of the new model.
