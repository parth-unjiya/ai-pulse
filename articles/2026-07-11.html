    The release of LingBot-VA 2.0 by Ant Group's Robbyant marks a significant advancement in Physical AI, with a focus on embodiment and foresight reasoning. The introduction of LingBot-VA 2.0 could signal a competitive shift in the AI research landscape, as companies like Ant Group invest heavily in developing AI models that can interact more effectively with the physical world.
